IRHS BRidge Day
April 18th, 2019
What is BRidge Day?
We are proud to present BRidge Day: a full day conference at Iroquois Ridge High School designed to help Grade 8 students bridge the gap between elementary school and high school.
Students will attend various workshop sessions focused on promoting and addressing their wellbeing and key needs under four main pillars: Ready, Set, Go, and Ridge!
Students will also have the opportunity to meet current high school students and teachers, as well as learn about the extracurricular opportunities available at the Ridge. We want these future Trailblazers to become involved with our school and community.
We look forward to meeting all the grade 8 students who will be joining us in September 2019!

Workshops
Workshops are filled on a first come, first served basis.
Pillar 1 Workshops - "Ready"
Mind Your Mind
          Does coming to high school sound stressful to you? Come to our Mind Your Mind workshop to learn about some of the ways to relieve stress and handle the heavy workload that comes with being in high school. Join us as we explore healthy ways to deal with stress with activities, such as Just Dance and Paint Your Vibe.

Paws-itivity!
          Do you want to meet cute dogs and learn ways to relax? Come to our workshop to hang out with therapy dogs! Here at the Ridge, therapy dogs are brought in the week before final exams to help students cope with stress. During this workshop, future Ridge students can come & experience the same benefits!
* Please be warned that if you signed up for this workshop you may be redirected on the day of due to spacing issues.

Groove Your Way to Wellness
          Destress with Zumba! Come to this fun and active workshop that allows you to get up and move. Incorporating wellness into active living through Zumba! Come out, get up and dance it out.
Pillar 2 Workshops - "Set"
Seek Your Summit
          Want to improve your public speaking skills? Want to build your self-confidence? Then "Seek Your Summit" is for you! Through this interactive workshop you will be able build your debate and social skills!

All for One and One for BRidge
          Want to compete alongside a team to win it all? In this fun game of minute to win it, students will have to work together to be successful. This will help students develop many team building skills and test their competitive edge. Teams will be challenged to come together and use various teamwork strategies. Come to the "All for one and one for Bridge" " workshop to have some fun and meet new friends!

Equality. Diversity. Empowerment
          The workshop will be divided into three sections and will have interactive activities with media aspects incorporated. Topics that will be discussed include background, gender, sexuality, etc.

Digital Respect
          Love the internet? Want to know ways to keep yourself safe online? The "Digital Respect" workshop will change the way you deal with online safety and teach you ways to deal with cyberbullying.
Pillar 3 - "Go"
The Art of High School
          Interested in finding ways to get involved with the arts at the Ridge? This workshop will consist of fun interactive games and activities that reflect the arts programs offered. Come out to meet some of the fine artists of our school and learn how our school can help you advance your artistic abilities.

40 Hours to Save the World
          Are you wondering how to get your 40 hours? Do you want your impact to reach beyond the walls of high school? This workshop will go over various ways for you to get involved in service work at the Ridge and to get those 40 volunteer hours that you need.

Now Seeking: Ridge Leaders In Action
          If you are an enthusiastic individual who loves connecting with your school community and meeting new people, this workshop is for you! Join us and discover the various leadership opportunities available at the Ridge. Enhancing and demonstrating leadership at Iroquois Ridge is only possible through students involvement and dedication. By sharing ideas, leaders at the ridge continue to make our school a better place for the Ridge family. Come and experience what it's like to be a leader at the Ridge!
Pillar 4 - "Ridge"
Every student will be placed in a Ridge 101 workshop, which will be a Q&A session with experienced high school students and teachers.
