What is BRidge Day?
We are proud to present BRidge Day: a full day conference at Iroquois Ridge High School designed to help Grade 8 students bridge the gap between elementary school and high school.
Students will attend various workshop sessions focused on promoting and addressing their wellbeing and key needs under four main pillars: Ready, Set, Go, and Ridge!
Students will also have the opportunity to meet current high school students and teachers, as well as learn about the extracurricular opportunities available at the Ridge. We want these future Trailblazers to become involved with our school and community.
We look forward to meeting all the grade 8 students who will be joining us in September 2019!

Workshops
Workshops are filled on a first come, first served basis.
Community Crawl
 
         Have you heard about the 40 hour volunteer requirement? Do you want to find out different ways that you can volunteer? This workshop will go over various ways for you to get involved in service work at the Ridge. You will be competing in a run-through of different volunteering activities!                      

Leader of the Wolf Pack

          Want to start your own club, or become the next Prime Minister of Canada? Whatever goal or motivation you may have in mind, leadership skills are beneficial, valuable and admirable assets for anyone. Even if you are just curious about what makes a strong leader, then come join us for a fun, interactive workshop filled with activities and games run by some of our OSLC and Link Crew student leaders. Learn about leadership here at the Ridge and find the leader inside of YOU!                     
The Ridge: Your Support Community
 
         High school can be stressful! This workshop teaches you how to manage day-to-day stresses and conflicts by exploring personal, real-life situations - as shared by your future peers. We navigate a variety of topics ranging from managing school work to social acceptance, to LGBTQ+ issues and in-school resources. This practical workshop is facilitated by Ridge Pride, Ridge United, and the Wellness Team.                     

Rowdy Ridge Relay
 
          Do you love spirit? Do you want to get a taste of what athletics are like at the ridge? Join us for an incredible active relay where every part of ridge athletics will be incorporated!                     

The Art of High School
 
         Interested in finding ways to getting involved with the arts at the Ridge? This workshop will consist of fun interactive games and activities that reflect the arts at the Ridge. Come out to meet some of the fine artists of our school and learn how our school can help you advance your artistic abilities.                      

Tech At the Ridge
 
          Innovation, STEM, and creativity are all the things the Iroquois Ridge tech clubs are about. If you want to learn more about technology at the Ridge and play some fun interactive activities, then come to our workshop!                      

Steps to Success
 
          Come to this workshop to learn about the different academic pathways and supports at the Ridge! From the difference between an academic vs an applied course, what a clustered course is, how to pick the right courses for university/college, how to get free tutoring within the school, and the kind of guidance offered in the PCC, we will cover everything!
